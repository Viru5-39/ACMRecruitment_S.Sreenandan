Wolf, Goat, and Cabbage River Crossing Solution

1.Take the Goat across. The farmer first rows the goat to the destination side.
2.Return alone. The farmer rows back empty-handed.
3.Take the Wolf across. The farmer brings the wolf to the destination side.
4.Return with the Goat. To prevent disaster, the farmer brings the goat back to the starting side.
5.Take the Cabbage across. The farmer leaves the goat and rows the cabbage to the destination side, leaving it with the wolf.
6.Return alone. The farmer rows back empty-handed one last time.
7.Take the Goat across. The farmer makes the final trip with the goat.

Everyone finally crosses the river safely
