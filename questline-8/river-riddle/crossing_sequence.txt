Wolf, Goat, and Cabbage River Crossing Solution

1.Take the wolf across. The farmer first rows the wolf to the destination side.
2.Return alone. The farmer rows back empty-handed.
3.Take the cabbage across. The farmer brings the cabbage to the destination side, leaving wolf and cabbage together at the destination side.
4.Return alone. The farmer rows back empty handed.
5.Take the Goat across. The farmer makes the final trip with the Goat.

Everyone finally crosses the river safely
